# Budget & Shifts

A friendly, mobile-first personal tracker that unifies two everyday logs in one app:
money in/out against budget categories, and the job shifts you work.

**Register:** product (design serves the task — fast, calm, daily-use utility).

## Who & where

One person, on their phone, logging a coffee on the bus or a shift after clocking
out. Quick, one-handed, low-friction. Glanceable progress, never anxiety-inducing.

## Principles

- **Effortless capture.** One `+`, one sheet, an In / Out / Shift toggle. Smart
  defaults (today, last-used category/employer, now) so the happy path is one number.
- **Encouraging, not judgmental.** Progress bars and a clear monthly balance; rose
  brand color keeps it light.
- **Two independent sections.** Shifts and transactions never auto-link; the user
  stays in control.

## Surfaces

- **Budget** — month switcher, net summary, category cards with progress.
- **Shifts** — weekly groups, hours summary.
- **Add sheet** — the core capture flow.
- **Settings** — categories, employers, account.
- **Sign-in** — magic link.
